Introduction to Digital India

Digital India is a flagship initiative launched by the Government of India to transform the country into a digitally empowered society and knowledge economy. The program focuses on digital infrastructure, online governance, and citizen empowerment through technology.

Vision and Objectives

  • Provide high-speed internet connectivity
  • Deliver government services digitally
  • Ensure digital identity for citizens
  • Promote digital literacy

Major Digital India Services

  • Aadhaar: Digital identity system
  • DigiLocker: Online document storage
  • BHIM UPI: Digital payment platform
  • UMANG: One-stop government service app
  • SWAYAM: Online education platform
