What is Digital India?

Digital India is a flagship programme launched by the Government of India in 2015. Its main aim is to transform India into a digitally empowered society by using information technology to deliver services efficiently and transparently.

Vision of Digital India

The vision of Digital India is to ensure that digital infrastructure becomes a core utility for every citizen, governance and services are available on demand, and citizens are digitally empowered.

Main Objectives

  • High-speed internet access for all
  • Digital identity for every citizen
  • Paperless and cashless governance
