Major Digital Services

Aadhaar

Aadhaar provides a unique digital identity to Indian residents, enabling easy access to government schemes and services.

DigiLocker

DigiLocker allows citizens to store and access important documents such as certificates and licenses in digital format.

BHIM UPI

BHIM UPI promotes cashless transactions by enabling instant bank-to-bank payments through mobile phones.

UMANG App

UMANG integrates multiple government services into a single mobile application.
