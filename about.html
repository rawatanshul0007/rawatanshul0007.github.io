What is Digital India?

Digital India is a flagship programme launched by the Government of India in 2015. Its main aim is to transform India into a digitally empowered society by using technology for governance, services, and citizen empowerment.

Vision of Digital India

The vision of Digital India is to ensure that digital infrastructure becomes a core utility for every citizen and that government services are available on demand through digital platforms.

Objectives

  • High-speed internet access
  • Digital identity for citizens
  • Paperless governance
  • Digital literacy for all
