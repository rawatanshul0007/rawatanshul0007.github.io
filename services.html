Major Services under Digital India

Aadhaar: Provides a unique digital identity to citizens, enabling easy access to government schemes and services.

DigiLocker: Allows citizens to store and access important documents such as certificates and licenses digitally.

BHIM UPI: Promotes cashless transactions through fast and secure digital payments.

UMANG App: A single platform that provides access to multiple government services.

SWAYAM: An online education platform offering free courses to students and learners.
