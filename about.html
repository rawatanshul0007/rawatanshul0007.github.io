What is Digital India?

Digital India is a national programme launched to ensure that government services are available to citizens electronically by improving online infrastructure and increasing internet connectivity across the country.

Vision of Digital India

The vision of Digital India is to transform India into a digitally empowered society and knowledge economy where every citizen can access services online in an affordable and secure manner.

Objectives

  • Digital infrastructure as a core utility
  • Governance and services on demand
  • Digital empowerment of citizens
