Impact on Indian Society

Digital India has improved transparency in governance, reduced corruption, increased access to education and healthcare, and boosted digital payments.

Key Achievements

  • 80+ crore internet users
  • 130+ crore Aadhaar cards issued
  • Rapid growth of UPI and fintech startups
  • Government services available online

Impact on Students & Youth

Students benefit from online education platforms, digital certificates, and easy access to learning resources.
